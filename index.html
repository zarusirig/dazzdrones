How It Works

SEO in plain English

Google needs to do four things well:

1) FindDiscover pages via links + sitemap.
2) ReadUnderstand the page content instantly.
3) TrustBelieve you're credible (mentions, links).
4) RankChoose you for the best search match.
Our job

Remove barriers to "Read", then build content + authority so Google confidently ranks you.

Use this slide as the anchor for later: if "Read" fails (site isn't crawl-readable), nothing else matters.

Structural Issue

Two sites = split SEO power

When content is on one host and the main product experience is on another, Google can treat them as separate "properties". That dilutes authority and slows growth.

Main Product Site Booking + service pages
The fix

Consolidate SEO landing pages onto the main domain so authority, links, and relevance all stack in one place.

Analogy: "Like building two shops on separate streets and splitting your reviews between them."
